About Poor Bunny

Game Story

Poor Bunny is a thrilling endless runner game where you take on the role of a cute bunny trying to escape from various dangers. Jump over obstacles, avoid enemies, and collect coins to unlock new characters and power-ups!

Features

  • Simple one-touch controls
  • Multiple unlockable characters
  • Power-ups and special abilities
  • Global leaderboards
  • Daily challenges and rewards

How to Play

  • Tap or click to make the bunny jump
  • Jump over obstacles and gaps to keep running
  • Collect coins to unlock new characters and power-ups
  • Avoid enemies and dangerous traps
  • Complete daily challenges for special rewards
